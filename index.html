Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you dispatch an ambulance?
We strive to dispatch an ambulance within 15 minutes of receiving a request in urban areas. Response times may vary in remote locations.
Do you provide inter-hospital transfers?
Yes, our ambulances are available for both emergency and scheduled inter-hospital patient transfers.
Are your ambulances equipped with advanced medical technology?
Our fleet includes Basic Life Support (BLS) and Advanced Life Support (ALS) ambulances equipped with state-of-the-art medical equipment and staffed by trained paramedics.
Can I book an ambulance in advance?
Yes, you can schedule ambulance services in advance by filling out the booking form or contacting our support team.
